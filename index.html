About

Dr. BKN Sudha is amicable and friendly to the patients to understand their concerns and guide them in a simple, sophisticated, advanced way of treatment and she attends various gynecologist conferences to update the newer and better way of practice Sudha Hospital is established to cater the needs of the women during their planning pregnancy, pregnancy, delivery, menstrual irregularities, family planning,menopausal symptoms, teenage issues and cancer screening. Dr Sudha has 11 years of vast experience in this field. She delivers individualized care and focused management after detailed counseling. She did MBBS in SVS Medical College, Mahbubnagar, DGO in Osmania Medical College, DNB in RDT Hospital. She did fellowship in infertility and brought up smiles into many couples who were blessed with healthy babies. She did CIMP course in Bangalore to cater the needs of menopausal women. She follows ethical practice with evidence based management. She updates herself with webinars and journal
